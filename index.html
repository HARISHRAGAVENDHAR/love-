About Us

Simplifying Indirect Taxation & Trade Regulations

Don Associates was founded by Don Bosco in 2011 and is a specialised advisory firm dedicated to simplifying the complexities of indirect taxation and international trade regulations for modern businesses. We hold deep domain expertise across GST, Customs, EXIM, and legacy tax laws.

What makes us stand apart is that we deliver clear, practical guidance that enables our clients to operate with confidence and ease. When disputes arise, our litigation team represents clients across all judicial and quasi-judicial forums — from original authorities to appellate bodies, tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court.

Beyond advisory, Don Associates is committed to strengthening the larger tax ecosystem through knowledge sharing and capacity building. To us, integrity and trust mean the world.
